Keep It Fresh

Fabric Care

🌊
Wash Cold
Always wash Flying Machine denim in cold water (30°C max) to preserve colour, texture, and stretch. Turn inside out before washing.
🚫
No Tumble Dry
Avoid machine drying — heat damages premium denim fibres. Hang dry in shade for best results and longer garment life.
🧴
Mild Detergent Only
Use a mild, denim-safe detergent. Avoid bleach and harsh chemicals that can strip fabric dye and weaken fibres over time.
🔲
Iron Low Heat
If ironing is needed, use low heat and iron inside out. For shirts, use medium steam. Never iron directly on prints or embroidery.

Got Questions?

Frequently Asked

What is Flying Machine's return policy? ↓
Flying Machine offers a 30-day hassle-free exchange and return policy across all stores and online orders. Items must be unused, unwashed, and in original packaging with tags intact.
How do I find the right size? ↓
Use our size chart above to match your measurements. If you're between sizes, we recommend sizing up for comfort. You can also visit any Flying Machine store for a free in-store fitting session.
Are Flying Machine products available online? ↓
Yes! Shop the full Flying Machine collection on our official website and all major e-commerce platforms including Myntra, Flipkart, and Amazon. Online-exclusive styles are also available periodically.
What fabrics does Flying Machine use? ↓
Flying Machine uses premium ring-spun denim, stretch cotton blends, and high-quality twills sourced from Arvind Mills — one of India's largest textile manufacturers. All fabrics are tested for comfort, durability, and colour fastness.
Do you offer alteration services? ↓
Yes — alteration services including hemming, waist adjustments, and sleeve shortening are available at all Flying Machine stores. Visit our Services page for full details and pricing.
